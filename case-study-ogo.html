The Challenges

OGO app founders reached us to redesign and redefine user experience for the app that will ensure
passenger safety while we look to design for positive riding experience with the app. The challenges are to
find out how to make the app not only user friendly but as companion to the passengers.

The design and UX was above our expectations.
The usability and clarity of the solution is a key factor in
on-the-go taxi booking to date.

- OGO

The Solutions

We designed user experience by keep all the user needs on the table to priority and define their pain points
while managing business goal are met. Provided a view interfaces of all the services required for a journey in
step by step access until their journey completion with fully integrated on a map view with safety features when user in need.

Be Happy when
you're on go in
OGO

Now assam people travel anywhere happily within the
city or Outstation. We created user interfaces for the
people to make choices to move on map view. Interactions
that allow users to switch the travel plan from one to another.
As a third dimension to the business goal and user goal,
OGO is designed to provide delivery services on the same
map view that makes the life easier to track your
delivery orders.

Feel a ‘safe journey’

In today’s world, woman safety is a concern and to deal it we brought well tested placement of the
button to make sure the number one priority of the app. At the same time SOS button not be meant to be
tapped other wise. We have smartly tackled the problem by providing the button within the access range of user’s thumb.

Pleasant journey -
Inside city or
Outstation

App view is visualized such a way user travel is followed by a
bird that follows from the beginning to end of the journey.
Clearly displayed taxis nearby on the map that shows the on
glance view of a availability and waiting time. Enter where to
go with smart assistance to fill automatically and take to the
brid view on the map. View whole journey estimations with
time & cost that strategically priced by market study.
All interaction in on the screen makes use focus and complete
the task. All new interfaces for commuting to outstation with the
same user experience as inside city travel.

Recall all stories
with saved Histories
and Favorites

All your stories of your every journey is saved to a history
that shalled view in a memorable listing pages designs
and details screen designs. User will be all set to go with
OGO with one simple tap as previous journey is saved and
presented at appropriate time and placement on
app screen.

Well! Payment will be made for sure,
because it’s well designed

Business can only run and succeed when payment is made properly. Usability has to be
the focus on these screen designs. We work on several options to test and validate to design which was a
human-centered design approach. Well, when payment screens are well designs payment will be made by users without fail.

Designs for Drivers

All the the interactions designed for the drivers app will friendly
enough for Drivers who are mostly not high academic
background. Hence we have interfaces for the which very
intuitive and useable with any ambiguity. In generally there
interfaces are designed with large sizes of iconography and easy
to understand. We understood about how a day in life of taxi driver
goes on and removed distracting features from the app that
ensures the safety of the drivers.
